About

I am a freelancer who focuses on digital illustration. The world of pictures is something that really makes me interesting because with an illustration, we can see the picture of an idea from every work of someone's imagination that is described.

Illustrations are pictures that clarify the idea of ​​a story or narrative. The function of the illustration is to bring a story to life. The object of the illustration can be in the form of humans, plants, and animals, which are adapted to the theme of the story being made.
